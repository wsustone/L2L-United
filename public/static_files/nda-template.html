Non-Circumvent & Non-Disclosure Agreement

This template captures the baseline commitment for Lifetime to Lifetime United collaborations. Complete the party details, review the terms, and sign digitally using the panel below before exporting to PDF or printing.

Instructions
  1. Fill in the parties, effective date, and any transaction notes.
  2. Have each participant sign using the canvas or by typing their legal name as a digital signature.
  3. Click Print / Save as PDF to generate a signed copy. Upload the finalized PDF to the portal.

Agreement Summary

The parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information shared in connection with the Lifetime to Lifetime United program and to refrain from circumventing one another in transactions introduced through that relationship.

  • All confidential materials are provided solely for evaluation of potential collaborations.
  • The recipient will not disclose, reproduce, or use the information except as expressly permitted by this agreement.
  • The parties will not circumvent or bypass one another for opportunities originating from shared introductions or materials.
  • The obligations in this agreement will remain in effect for three (3) years from the Effective Date unless a superseding contract is executed.

Transaction Notes

Summarize the opportunity or scope of discussions covered by this NDA (optional). This field is provided for clarity when multiple NDAs are executed.
